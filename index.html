Spring Academic Marathon is happening now!

Fill out this form every time you complete an assignment, quiz, midterm, or project to earn points for your cluster in the competition. The form will close at 7:30 p.m. on March 19th, so get everything in as soon as possible!

Point System:
100 points: Completing midterms, large projects
50 points: Problem sets, completed lab reports, completed essays
20 points: Lab/essay/project checkpoints, reading responses, prelabs
10 points: Discussion/lecture attendance, discussion posts
