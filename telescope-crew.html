Telescope Crew

About Telescope Crew

You can join Telescope Crew at the start of every school year. In the fall, we will train Telescope Crew members on the Schmidt-Cassegrain and Newtonian telescopes.

In the spring, we begin Sidewalk Astronomy, where we bring the Newtonian telescopes to Sproul Plaza and teach the local community about astronomy, allowing them to stargaze and look through the telescopes.

Spring is also when training for the Richard Treffers dome telescope will occur (by application only).

Apply to be trained on the Astronomy Department's dome telescope, Treffers, by February 27, 2026!

Come to Sidewalk Astronomy and star parties to raise your chances of being accepted.
