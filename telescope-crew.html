Telescope Crew

About Telescope Crew

You can join Telescope Crew at the start of every school year. In the fall, we train members on the Schmidt-Cassegrain and Newtonian telescopes.

In the spring, we begin Sidewalk Astronomy, bringing telescopes to Sproul Plaza to teach the community about astronomy.

Spring is also when training for the Richard Treffers dome telescope occurs (by application only).

Apply to be trained on the Astronomy Department's dome telescope, Treffers, by February 27, 2026!

Come to Sidewalk Astronomy and star parties to raise your chances of being accepted.
